## How to Use

1. Run the migration file: `supabase/migrations/014_meetings_calendar_enhancements.sql`
2. Go to the Meetings page in your app
3. Scroll to "Standard Stake Meetings" section
4. Click "Add" on any template to create a meeting with default settings
5. Customize the meeting time, recurrence, and permissions as needed

All meetings are based on current General Handbook guidelines and can be customized to fit your stake's specific needs and schedule.
