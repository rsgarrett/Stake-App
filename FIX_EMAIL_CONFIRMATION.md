# Fix "Email not confirmed" Error

## Option 1: Disable Email Confirmation (Easiest for Development)

1. Go to: https://supabase.com/dashboard/project/gqqbllsbukcipczrdjma
2. Click **Authentication** → **Providers** → **Email**
3. Scroll down to **"Confirm email"** section
4. **Uncheck** "Enable email confirmations"
5. Click **Save**

Now you can log in without confirming your email!

## Option 2: Manually Confirm Your Email

1. Go to: https://supabase.com/dashboard/project/gqqbllsbukcipczrdjma
2. Click **Authentication** → **Users**
3. Find your email (rsgarrett@gmail.com)
4. Click the **three dots** (⋮) next to your user
5. Click **"Confirm email"** or **"Confirm user"**

## Option 3: Check Your Email

If email confirmations are enabled, check your inbox (and spam folder) for a confirmation email from Supabase and click the confirmation link.

---

**Recommended:** Use Option 1 for development - it's the fastest way to get started!
